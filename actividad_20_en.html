Activity 20: Aviary - Cartagena

20- How to experience the Aviary? The National Aviary of Cartagena is an ambitious project, developed to showcase Colombia's incredible diversity of birdlife. The facility, which spans 7 hectares, is the largest in the Americas and the sixth largest in the world, and is home to over 1,800 birds from more than 138 different species. Impressive, right? Marvel at the imposing Andean condor -Colombia’s national bird-, enjoy the spectacular tropical birds with their multicolored plumage, like the scarlet macaws, the vibrant green jay, and the beautiful blue-winged tanager. Or if you happen to have a thing for the retro tropical vibes of flamingos...you're in luck! There's an entire flock of them! But the park isn’t just about the birds. It’s also a fantastic showcase of Colombian flora and fauna, playing an important role in conservation and education, especially for many species that are endangered. It’s a perfect half-day trip for just about everyone: kids love it, bird lovers are fascinated, nature enthusiasts are delighted, and influencers looking for photo-worthy moments? They love it too. In short – it’s a hit with everyone!
