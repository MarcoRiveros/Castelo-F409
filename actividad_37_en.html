Activity 37: Bazurto Market - Cartagena -

37- Bazurto Market: This vibrant and lively market is the culinary heart of Cartagena, where Caribbean flavors are enjoyed in their most authentic form. Embark on a group walking tour through Bazurto Market and discover the vibrant culture and flavors of Cartagena. This immersive experience takes you off the beaten path to explore a traditional market, where you'll encounter the sights, sounds, and tastes of Colombian cuisine. While enjoying a refreshing glass of Guarapo, a cold beer, and other regional treats, you'll learn about the market's history and its importance to the city's economy, while experiencing the daily life of the locals.
