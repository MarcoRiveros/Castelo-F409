Activity 43: The National Aviary of Colombia - Cartagena -

43-Come and experience an unforgettable adventure at one of the most impressive bird sanctuaries on the continent. The National Aviary of Colombia is a true natural gem. Be amazed by the majestic Andean condor, Colombia's national bird, and a burst of color from the scarlet macaws, the brilliant green aracari, and the charming blue-winged tanager. But this place is more than just about birds: it's also a space dedicated to conservation, environmental education, and the preservation of endangered species. In addition, you will be able to appreciate the richness of Colombian flora and fauna in a carefully designed natural setting. Ideal for a half-day excursion, the Aviary delights both young and old: curious children, bird lovers, nature explorers, and even influencers in search of the perfect photo. It's a plan no one wants to miss!
