Activity 17: La Serrezuela in Cartagena

17-Discover how La Serrezuela blends the cultural essence of Cartagena with fine dining and luxury shopping. The former Circo Teatro, located in the traditional San Diego neighborhood, has been reborn as the region’s most important center for culture, entertainment, commerce, and gastronomy, establishing itself as the new icon of Cartagena’s Historic Center. The commercial space surrounding the iconic bullring offers visitors a unique experience where, beyond exclusive shops, they can enjoy events, exhibitions, and gatherings that highlight the richness of Caribbean culture.La Serrezuela boasts an unparalleled culinary offering, perfect for those seeking authentic flavors in a setting full of history and elegance. Visiting La Serrezuela means stepping into a meeting place where art, music, culture, and entertainment come together in perfect harmony. We look forward to welcoming you.
