Activity 29: Providencia - Cartagena -

29-Providencia is an island that maintains its wild and authentic charm, remaining one of Colombia's best-kept secrets. With its stunning natural beauty, delicious seafood, and traditional beachside horse races, this island is a peaceful and enchanting retreat. The endless colors of its surface waters, the vibrant underwater landscapes, and the green mangroves that line the shore create a unique ecosystem, home to tiny, colorful fish. Itâ€™s the perfect destination for ecotourism lovers and those interested in aquatic activities like diving and snorkeling, thanks to its remarkable coral reefs and the rich biodiversity beneath its crystal-clear waters. Additionally, Providencia is home to the Old Providence Natural Park, a Sea Flower Biosphere Reserve declared by UNESCO in 2000. What truly makes Providencia special, however, is its people. The islanders are warm, cheerful, and passionate about music and cuisine, always ready to share the secrets and magic of island life with visitors.
