Activity 5: Surfing in Cartagena

5-Nestled on Colombia’s Caribbean coast, Cartagena is a vibrant city know for its colonial charm and lively culture. Beyond its historical appeal, it’s also an excellent kitesurfing destination.
In nearby Manzanillo del Mar beach, you'll find consistent winds, flat water to small waves, and a quiet, uncrowded beach — perfect for kiting freely without obstacles. The prime kitesurfing season runs from December to April, with steady wind speeds averaging 15 to 20 knots.
