Activity 49: Cartagena and Its Museums

49-In Cartagena de Indias, Colombia, you'll find a wide range of museums that explore various aspects of the city's and the country's history and culture. Some of the most notable include the Zenú Gold Museum, the Historical Museum of Cartagena, the Caribbean Naval Museum, the Museum of Modern Art of Cartagena, and the Rafael Núñez House Museum.
