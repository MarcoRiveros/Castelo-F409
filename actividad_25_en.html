Activity 25: Experience Traditional Fishing in Cartagena

25-Experience Traditional Fishing in La Boquilla! Just minutes from Cartagena, the charming fishing village of La Boquilla invites you to dive into a truly local adventure and discover the art of traditional fishing. The warm Caribbean waters are home to species like snook, tarpon, mojarra, black snapper, and mullet, along with tasty treasures such as sea snails, clams, shrimp, crabs, and lobsters. Join a fishing tour with local fishermen, who will guide you through the mangroves and coastal waters in traditional canoes, sharing their time-honored techniques and deep knowledge of the sea. The sun-drenched shores and rocky outcrops surrounding Cartagena make this area a true paradise for saltwater fishing lovers. It’s an authentic, relaxing experience—perfect for connecting with nature and local culture!
