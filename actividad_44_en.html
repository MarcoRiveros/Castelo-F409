Activity 44: Safari in Cartagena

44- UTV Safari: Explore Wildlife and Mountains in a Nature Reserve: Immerse yourself in the heart of conservation at a regenerative nature reserve near Cartagena. The UTV safari offers an exciting way to explore the tropical dry forest of the reserve. Navigate vibrant trails surrounded by mountains and discover rich wildlife, including cotton-top tamarins, red howler monkeys, capuchins, macaws, parrots, and various species of exotic birds. By joining this adventure, you directly contribute to the foundation's mission to preserve ecosystems, empower local farmers, and protect biodiversity. Stunning views, native animals, and fresh tropical fruits await you, making this experience unforgettable. Additionally, this sanctuary is dedicated to rehabilitating and releasing parrots and macaws rescued from illegal trafficking, giving them a second chance at freedom.
