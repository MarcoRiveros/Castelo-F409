Activity 16: Colonial Architecture in Cartagena

16-Let yourself be carried away by the charming streets of Cartagena and discover its stunning colonial architecture. It's the perfect plan for a romantic evening stroll through the historic center, accompanied by the people you love. As you explore the “Corralito de Piedra,” you'll admire colonial churches, boutique shops, iconic buildings, and magical, history-filled alleys. The walled city, with its picturesque corners and nostalgic atmosphere, sets the perfect scene for a dreamy horse-drawn carriage ride through the heart of Cartagena’s historic center.
