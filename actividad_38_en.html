Activity 38: A Carriage Ride Through Cartagena de Indias

38- A carriage ride through Cartagena de Indias is a unique experience to explore the historic center, filled with history and colonial charm. It’s an original way to wander through the cobblestone streets and plazas of the walled city. The ride mainly takes you through the historic center, known for its colorful streets, flower-adorned balconies, and colonial buildings. Carriages are available at various points in the historic center, especially near Plaza de Bolívar and Plaza de San Pedro.
