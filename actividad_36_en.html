Activity 36: Tierra Bomba Island - Cartagena -

36- Tierra Bomba Island: It is the closest island to Cartagena, in fact, it is located right in front of the city's bay, just 10 minutes by boat from the La Bodeguita dock. The island is divided into 4 sectors: Tierra Bomba, Punta Arena, Bocachica, and Caño Loro. -Barú Island: It is probably the most popular and visited island in Cartagena. Barú is a peninsula with beautiful white sandy beaches and turquoise waters.
