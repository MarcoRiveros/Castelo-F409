Activity 10: Rosario Islands - Cartagena

10-If you're in Cartagena and looking for beaches in more natural settings, away from the tourist crowds, a great option is the Rosario Islands. This charming archipelago, made up of about 28 islands, is located less than an hour away by boat from the city. Many companies in Cartagena offer this service with various tour packages. The Rosario Islands are home to white sandy beaches and crystal-clear waters, perfect for relaxing and enjoying nature.
