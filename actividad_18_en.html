Activity 18: Aquarium of Isla del Rosario - Cartagena

18-Marvel at the aquatic treasures of the Rosario Island Aquarium. The Rosario Islands Aquarium is a magical place where you can admire the underwater treasures of the Caribbean Sea. You’ll be amazed by the variety and beauty of the species that inhabit it, such as sea turtles, angelfish, manta rays, and many more. The experience begins with a walk through the underwater tunnel, offering visitors a unique view of the aquarium from the ocean’s depths. Then, you’ll have the opportunity to explore several interactive exhibits that highlight everything you need to know about this incredibly rich and diverse marine life. Additionally, an instructor on-site hosts a dolphin show, where tourists can swim with the dolphins or take a memorable photo.
