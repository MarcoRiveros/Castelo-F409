Activity 6: Carmona Museum in Cartagena

6-Carmona Museum, another gem of Cartagena in the heart of the Historic Center. Just a few meters from the walled city, right on Second Street in the area known as Playa de la Artillería or Cuartel de la Artillería in Cartagena’s Historic Center, lies the Carmona Museum a tribute to the everyday life of the Caribbean and, of course, to the Heroic City of Cartagena de Indias.
