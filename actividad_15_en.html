Activity 15: Learn to Cook Like a Local in Cartagena

Don't just indulge in Colombian cuisine—learn to cook like a local with the help of a professional local chef in one of the many cooking classes in Cartagena. Your instructor will guide you and your fellow participants through the process of preparing appetizers, main courses, and desserts, equipping you with everything you need to recreate these dishes at home. Once the course is complete, everyone will sit down together to enjoy the creations in a dream-like atmosphere.
