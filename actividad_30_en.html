Activity 30: Urban Art in Cartagena

30- Getsemaní, Urban Art in Cartagena: The vibrant streets of Cartagena de Indias have transformed into an open-air gallery, where every wall serves as a canvas filled with dreams and positive energy. Travelers from around the world have come to the city to discover this new wave of urban art, which has taken graffiti and street art to an exceptional level. Today, these works share the spotlight with the Historic Center and its stunning colonial structures, offering visitors a captivating collection of murals and street art pieces that spark the imagination of anyone strolling through the neighborhood. It's common to see tourists taking photos with these colorful and creative works that bring the streets of Getsemaní to life.
