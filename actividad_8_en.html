Activity 8: Scuba Diving in Cartagena de Indias

In the surroundings of Cartagena, the reefs of Barú, the nearby islands, and the Salmedina lighthouse feature coral formations that serve as natural barriers against the waves, making it possible to dive all year round. This ecosystem, known as the Underwater Jungle, is full of life and constant motion. You’ll find a wide variety of fish, crustaceans, and mollusks of different species. The seabed also hides several shipwrecks some intentionally sunk for diving purposes, others for unknown reasons as well as ancient cannons scattered throughout the area, and many more surprises waiting to be discovered.
