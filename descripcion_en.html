My apartment in Serena del Mar, Cartagena.

Enjoy an elegant apartment on the fourth floor, with an elevator, located in the exclusive gated community of Castelo-Serena del Mar.

This spacious and modern apartment offers comfort and style. The master bedroom features a double bed (trundle bed style), a private bathroom with a shower, and a large walk-in closet.

The apartment also has 2 full bathrooms, each with a hot water shower for your comfort.

The second bedroom is equipped with two beds, one of which is a trundle bed, and a large closet

The kitchen is fully equipped with appliances such as a coffee maker, microwave, toaster, and more, perfect for preparing your meals.

In the comfortable living room, you will find a large sofa bed and access to the private balcony, ideal for relaxing and enjoying the atmosphere.

The modern dining area has seating for four, along with three bar stools for added comfort.

The gated community offers excellent amenities, including pools for adults and children, a volleyball court, 24-hour security, and more.

Additionally, it has a private beach just 15 minutes away, with easy booking through an app.

Just a few steps from the apartment, you'll find restaurants, shops, pharmacies, and a vibrant nightlife, allowing you to immerse yourself in the charm and unique energy of Cartagena.
