Activity 4: With Botero’s "Gordita" (Chubby Lady)

4-Welcome to La Gorda Gertrudis. This sculpture, located in Santo Domingo Square in the historic center of Cartagena, is a bronze statue of a voluptuous nude woman. The statue is a creation of Fernando Botero, a renowned Colombian painter and sculptor. He is famous for his depiction of robust or chubby people and animals in his artwork. Botero's creations can be admired in prominent locations around the world, and his distinctive artistic style is known as Boterismo. This beloved reclining figure has become a favorite spot for visitors to take photos. Famous local legends surround this statue; the most well-known says that touching La Gorda's breast will bring you luck in love.
